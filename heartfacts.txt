Prolonged use of technology devices like smartphones, laptops, and tablets can increase the risk of developing cardiovascular diseases. This is because prolonged use of these devices can lead to sedentary behavior, which is a risk factor for heart diseases.
Studies have shown that exposure to blue light emitted from electronic screens can interfere with the production of melatonin, a hormone that regulates sleep-wake cycles. Disrupting sleep patterns can negatively affect the heart health.
Using technology devices before bedtime can also interfere with the body's natural circadian rhythm, which can negatively affect the heart health.
